Kimchi Fried Rice

  • Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 Tablespoon
Day-old rice 4 cups
gochujang 3 Tablespoon
Rice Vinegar 1 Tablespoon
Scallions 5 (thinly sliced)
Mozzarella 3 ounces (grated)
Gruyere cheese 2 ounces (grated)
Cotija cheese 1 ounces (grated)
Kimchi 5 ounces (chopped and dried)

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Grease a 10-inch cast iron pan with 1 tablespoon (15g) butter, making sure to fully cover both the bottom and the sides.

Step 2 : In a medium mixing bowl, combine rice, gochujang, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sliced scallion whites. Using a flexible spatula, mix thoroughly.

Step 3 : Scrape half of rice mixture into buttered cast iron skillet and, using the bottom of a drinking glass or measuring cup, press down firmly to create a single even layer of seasoned rice. Distribute half of the mozzarella and Gruyère over the layer of rice, then scrape the rest of the rice mixture over grated cheese. Using the bottom of a drinking glass or measuring cup, press down firmly to create an even top layer of seasoned rice. Transfer pan to oven and cook for 35 minutes.

Step 4 :Remove pan from oven. Turn off oven and turn broiler on high. While broiler preheats, top rice with chopped kimchi. Distribute remaining Gruyère and mozzarella over kimchi and sprinkle Cotija over the other cheeses.

Step 5 : Place pan under broiler for about 2 minutes, or until Gruyère and mozzarella melt and bubble and Cotija begins to char in spots. Remove pan from under broiler, top with sliced scallion greens, and serve immediately.
